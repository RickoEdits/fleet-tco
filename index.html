How it's calculated: Finance cost uses a standard reducing-balance loan payment against the residual/balloon value. Fuel, service and tyre costs scale with monthly distance (contract distance ÷ term). Insurance is a monthly rate applied to the vehicle's cost. Pricing is indicative ex-showroom South Africa; fuel consumption figures are manufacturer-cycle estimates. Residual value, service/tyre rates and insurance are segment-level assumptions — open "Edit inputs" on any card to refine them for your own fleet data. Confirm final figures with your financier and dealership before signing.
