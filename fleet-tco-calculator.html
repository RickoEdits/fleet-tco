How it's calculated: Finance cost uses a standard reducing-balance loan payment against the residual/balloon value. Fuel, service and tyre costs scale with monthly distance (contract distance ÷ term). Insurance is a monthly rate applied to the vehicle's cost. Pricing is indicative ex-showroom South Africa. Fuel consumption is fixed to the manufacturer-cycle figure for each vehicle and can't be edited. Every other field — vehicle cost, RV/balloon, service & maintenance, tyres, licensing and insurance — is a starting estimate you can adjust to match your own fleet's quotes; service rates marked "● matched" come from real cost-per-km fleet data, "○ estimated" ones use a segment-level assumption. Confirm final figures with your financier and dealership before signing.
